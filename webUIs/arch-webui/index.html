Your Phone

Your phone is an exceptionally personzlied data capturing device.

This can be referred to as an Internet of Things (IoT) device. It has sensors to gather data about you.

Your Applications

In respect to this application if you only have an apple smart phone (os:13.0) you are provided with a pedometer, audio measurments & stand up time.

if you have an apple watch you are provided with heart rate, exercise & various activity data points.

If you use the LoseIt! app to track food intake, then your diet is closely measured.

The above methods and applications together form a well rounded digitized data set available to the masses.
